Problem

Solution

An n8n-orchestrated automation pipeline that pulls prospect data, prepares structured outreach drafts, generates audio via Voice RMV, stores files in Google Drive, and tracks status in real time.

Business Value

Reduced manual preparation time, faster outreach asset generation, organized file storage, and visible operational status for internal sales operations.

My Contribution

Business Context

Why prospect data needs more than a spreadsheet

Small and medium businesses collect prospect information through intake forms, spreadsheets, and manual lists. However, without a structured workflow, raw data stays inactive, outreach depends on manual effort, and follow-up consistency depends on individual discipline rather than system reliability.

3. Business Context

From Static List to Operational Pipeline

Most sales operations rely on a spreadsheet of names, phones, and notes. The list itself is not a system. It is a static artifact that depends entirely on human initiative to generate value.

The RMV Sales Engine V1 was designed to transform that static list into an operational pipeline. By combining n8n automation, Voice RMV for audio generation, Google Drive for organized storage, and a structured status tracking mechanism, the workflow turns raw prospect data into actionable outreach assets.

4. Problem Diagnosis
Strategic Thesis

"The prospect list is not the sales system. The workflow is what turns the list into execution."

The core problem is not the quality of the prospect data. The problem begins after collection, when raw information must be transformed into structured outreach, audio assets, organized files, and tracked status for follow-up.

Without automation, each of these steps depends on manual work, increasing preparation time, introducing inconsistency, and making it harder to maintain visibility over the sales pipeline.

person User Problem

The sales operator manually extracts data from spreadsheets, prepares messages, records audio, and stores files. There is no system. Every prospect starts from scratch.

storefront Business Problem

Without a structured pipeline, outreach becomes inconsistent, follow-up depends on memory, and the business cannot scale its sales operations without proportional headcount increase.

account_tree Operational Problem

There is no centralized status tracking, no automated file naming, no integration between data source and outreach preparation. Each step is disconnected from the next.
