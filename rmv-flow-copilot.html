bolt 02 / Product Snapshot

A quick overview of the Copilot, its operational purpose, core capabilities, and role within the ecosystem.

Operational Problem

Staff spend significant time searching for case information across scattered records, reconstructing context from messages and folders, and manually checking document status — all before they can help a client or move a case forward.

Product Approach

The Copilot connects staff to case context through a chat interface with controlled access to the management system. Instead of being a general AI writing tool, it is a workflow assistant that can answer questions, retrieve documents, summarize status, and organize next steps.

Core Capability

Multi-agent chat system with 21+ specialized agents for case status review, document checking, follow-up preparation, case summarization, checklist guidance, and human review preparation.

My Role

I designed the Copilot experience, agent structure, workflow integration, controlled data access patterns, and the separation between AI assistance and human decision-making.

03 / What the Copilot Solves

Staff need context, not just AI generated text.

In immigration case operations, every question a staff member answers requires context: case history, document status, previous follow-ups, pending actions. The Copilot reduces time spent searching and reconstructing by connecting AI assistance directly to structured operational data.

grid_view Operational Friction Points

search Context Recovery

Staff spend time searching across messages, emails, and folders to understand where a case stands before taking action.

description Document Tracking

Manually checking which documents were submitted, rejected, or still missing adds friction to every follow-up interaction.

forward_to_inbox Follow-up Burden

Crafting clear follow-up messages requires gathering scattered information and re-explaining what is missing or pending.

summarize Case Summarization

Reconstructing a complete case summary for internal review or handoff takes time and risks missing important context.

checklist Workflow Guidance

Without structured guidance, staff may follow inconsistent sequences or miss important steps in case processing.

verified_user Human Validation

Sensitive communication and case decisions still need human review, but preparing the information for that review can be inefficient without structured support.

The Copilot does not replace staff knowledge. It reduces the operational overhead of finding, organizing, and preparing case context — so staff can focus on the work that requires human judgment.

04 / Why a Dedicated AI Layer

The AI layer is separated from the management system by design.

The Copilot is not embedded inside the management system. It is a separate layer connected through a controlled MCP bridge. This separation means the AI interface can evolve independently, the management system stays focused on operational structure, and staff always have a clear boundary between AI-assisted work and direct system actions.

Independent Evolution

Design Choice

The Copilot can be updated, improved, and expanded without changing the management system. New agents, tools, and capabilities are added to the AI layer independently.

Result

Faster iteration without operational disruption

Controlled Access

Design Choice

The Copilot does not connect directly to the database. All data access goes through the MCP bridge, which exposes specific tools and approved actions instead of open-ended queries.

Result

AI works with data without unrestricted system access

Human Boundaries

Design Choice

The Copilot prepares, organizes, and retrieves information — but sensitive actions, communication, and case decisions remain under human control and review.

Result

AI assists without replacing human judgment

07 / Architecture & Integration

How the Copilot connects to the ecosystem.

The Copilot operates as an Electron-based desktop client with a multi-agent chat system. It connects to the Visa Flow MCP bridge to access case data, and through the MCP layer, it reaches the management system with controlled, approved actions.

dns

Desktop Client

Electron-based application that provides a dedicated chat interface for staff. It runs as a standalone desktop client connected to the ecosystem through the MCP layer.

psychology

Multi-Agent System

alt_route

MCP Bridge

All data requests from the Copilot go through the Visa Flow MCP layer, which exposes controlled tools and approved actions instead of giving the AI direct system access.
