🧪 Testing as an Operational Guardrail

As an AI-assisted systems and workflow builder, I know that a process is only as good as its quality control mechanism. In corporate operations, unverified AI outputs are not assets—they are operational risks.

To ensure that the Playbook-RMV framework delivers operational safety, I built a structured testing layer. This is where I transition from building the system to validating its reliability. Quality control is not an afterthought; it is integrated directly into the workflow to ensure that AI hallucinations or formatting errors never make it into the Corporate Knowledge Base without a strict human audit.
