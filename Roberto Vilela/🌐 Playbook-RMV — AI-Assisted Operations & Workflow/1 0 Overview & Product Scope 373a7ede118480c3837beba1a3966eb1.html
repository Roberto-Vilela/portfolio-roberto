1.0 Overview & Product Scope

The transition from isolated AI testing to governed corporate execution.

📜 1. The Manifest (Core Principles)

In corporate environments, AI value is frequently lost due to a lack of structure. When outputs are generated without traceability and accepted without validation, AI stops being a tool and becomes an operational risk.

The Playbook-RMV is built on three uncompromising principles:

  1. AI is an Assistant, not an Authority: AI accelerates data processing and logic generation, but human validation remains the ultimate source of truth.
  1. Experimentation must be an Asset: An experiment that lives only in a terminal log or a single employee's memory is a wasted resource. Knowledge must be captured, standardized, and searchable.
  1. Execution requires Telemetry: You cannot manage what you cannot measure. Every AI output must be linked to its hardware and model constraints to support cost predictability and resource accountability.

My bottom line: I believe in the difference between simply testing AI and truly operationalizing AI.

🚧 2. Scope Boundaries (What it IS and What it IS NOT)

To ensure strict risk mitigation and quality control, the boundaries of this workflow framework are clearly defined.

✅ IN SCOPE (What the framework does):

  • Acts as a Corporate Knowledge Base by saving prompts, model configurations, and validated responses into a structured local database (DuckDB).
  • Enforces Traceability by capturing and logging hardware telemetry (CPU, RAM, GPU/VRAM usage) for executions.
  • Operates under a strict Human-in-the-Loop Governance model, requiring human review before any output is considered a "validated asset".
  • Provides a visual and accessible interface to review historical test data.

❌ OUT OF SCOPE (What the framework explicitly prevents):

  • No Autonomous Execution: The system is not designed to deploy code or send communications automatically without human approval.
  • No "Black Box" Acceptance: The system does not accept AI outputs at face value. If an output contradicts the real-world evidence (e.g., terminal logs), the human operator corrects the course.
  • No Unmonitored Scaling: The system is not built to run massive batches of data blindly; it focuses on curated, audited, and repeatable workflow creation.

🎯 3. Success Metrics (How Value is Measured)

The success of Playbook-RMV is not measured by lines of code, but by its operational impact. The framework is designed to drive the following operational goals:

  • Maximized Knowledge Retention: Aimed at capturing and storing successful prompts and context configurations, transforming isolated tests into reusable assets and minimizing the "blank page" problem for future executions.
  • Consistent Traceability: Built to ensure that recorded outputs contain a snapshot of the model version and hardware telemetry used, supporting accurate cost forecasting and capacity planning.
  • Strict Governance Compliance: Focused on preventing AI hallucinations from becoming "accepted facts" by enforcing a mandatory human validation gate before any output is finalized.
