01 / The Problem

Knowledge Silos and Lost Context

When knowledge lives in individual minds rather than documented systems, it becomes vulnerable to loss when team members leave or projects pause.

Challenge

Knowledge Silos

Critical information exists only in individual minds or scattered across personal notes, making it hard to find and share.

Challenge

Lost Context

When experiments or workflows are paused, the context and reasoning behind decisions are often forgotten.

Challenge

Reinventing the Wheel

New team members or projects often repeat known experiments and decisions instead of building on prior knowledge.

02 / The Solution

Local Knowledge Base with AI-Assisted Search

A structured, offline-first knowledge system that captures workflows, experiments, and decisions for easy retrieval.
