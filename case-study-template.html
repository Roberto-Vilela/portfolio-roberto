Workflow Design Concept

DexaFit Wellness Hub

An AI-assisted customer retention concept designed to turn body scan results into a 90-day action loop.

person Role: Workflow Designer, Product Strategist
schedule Duration: 1 Week
build Tools: ChatGPT, Figma, Notion, Mermaid

summarize Executive Summary

Problem

Scan results are valuable but complex. Customers treat it as a one-time report instead of a continuous journey.

My Role

Mapped workflow, structured customer journey, defined AI-assisted interpretation layer.

Solution

A structured 90-day customer action loop bridging the gap between technical data and practical daily actions.

Impact

Designed to increase 90-day retention, encourage retesting, and reduce manual team follow-up.

Context / Background

DexaFit provides advanced body composition and metabolic testing. The challenge is that scan results can be valuable but also complex for customers to interpret. Without a structured follow-up system, customers may understand the scan as a one-time report instead of the beginning of a behavior-change journey.

The Core Problem

Customers receive valuable scan data, but without a guided action system, the business risks losing momentum, retention, and long-term customer engagement.

Discovery & Insights

Insight: Data isn't Action

Clients may not understand technical data. The solution needs to translate data into action.

update

Insight: Isolated Scans

An isolated scan doesn't create a habit. A retest needs to be planned from day 1 for a 90-day horizon.

gavel

Insight: AI Limits

AI can interpret trends but shouldn't diagnose alone. Human review is mandatory for safety.

The Core Strategy

Reposition the scan from a one-time result into the starting point of a 90-day action loop.

Solution & Architecture

[ Dashboard Mockup ]

1. The Customer Dashboard

Reduces decision friction by grouping scan results, goals, and the next recommended action in one place.

Design Decision

Used "Next Best Action" instead of a raw data dump to reduce cognitive overload for the user.

[ AI Review Flow Mockup ]

2. AI-Assisted Interpretation

AI drafts plain-language summaries of technical scan data. A human expert reviews and approves the summary before it reaches the customer.

Design Decision

Maintained human review because health data requires safety, compliance, and trust.

warning Challenges

As a conceptual independent project without internal company access, I had to rely on public data and AI-assisted market research. Additionally, dealing with health tech meant deliberately avoiding AI medical diagnosis to ensure conceptual compliance.

school Lessons Learned

This project reinforced that AI is most valuable when used to structure decisions, reduce friction, and support human judgment — not when it tries to replace expertise.
